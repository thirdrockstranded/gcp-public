# Case Study 2 — GCP Corporate Website

## Project
**Green Checkered Pants Infrastructure LLC** — corporate web presence

## Status
🔲 Pre-design. EIN obtained. Project not yet started.

## What It Is
A simple corporate website for Green Checkered Pants Infrastructure LLC. Serves as the public face of the LLC for prospective clients, partners, and general credibility purposes.

## Why It's Case Study 2
- Different domain than Quiltix (corporate/marketing vs. consumer tool)
- Simpler scope — lower risk for methodology failures
- Real deliverable for the LLC, not just an experiment
- Tests whether the methodology works on "real but simple" as well as "personal and complex"
- Opportunity to validate methodology on a project where the developer is also the primary stakeholder (no domain expert separation)

## Dual-Purpose Note
This project is simultaneously a real business deliverable and a methodology case study. That dual nature should be documented honestly — conclusions about methodology effectiveness may be colored by the developer's investment in the business outcome.

## Known Constraints (Preliminary)
- Static or near-static content at launch
- Must reflect LLC's actual service areas: small business web presence, Medicare broker management software, SAP APM consulting, AI agent development services
- Professional but not over-engineered — this is a credibility asset, not a product

## Open Questions (Design Phase Not Started)
- Stack: static site generator (Astro, Hugo) vs. simple React/Vite vs. plain HTML?
- Hosting: Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, Vercel?
- Domain: TBD
- Content: what pages, what copy, what calls to action?
